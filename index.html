Design

The Design of the Sparchunu Project is decided by you. We will do the best we can to make it align to your wants. In the case of incompatibility, new engines will be made... so watch out for new features!

Build

We all build your own projects. But we need to come together to build our own futures. With the Sparchunu project, this is an opportunity for everybody to improve, while also being able to give back to the community that has taugt us so much!

Launch

I launched the Sparchunu Project due to a need for new modern design language. the GNU Project is wonderful, but lacks the gorgeous design language people desire. By giving the average user what they want, the design language people want on the desktop happens.

Recent Works

Welcome to the Sparchunu Project. A New Beginning for Operating System Design, maintenance, and creation.

The founding of the Sparchunu Project was out of necessity. We need an operating system where we can do as we please. We need an operating system that isn't block our creativity every step of the way.

Community Identity

The Sparchunu Community is meant for those who desire a spot in the world of operating system use, development, and expansion. It is a commuinty meant to replace the overbearing and restricted operating systems from other large entities. And last but not least, it is a community meant to bring forth an generation to change the world of computing. Because "enough" isn't "enough" anymore.

Operating System Design

An operating system deserves the right to look good, and be functional. We have worked strenuously to give you the best performance and design possible from a new design language called "gorgeUS", a new replacement for the GTK and QT Frameworks, while still leaving compatibility for those libraries.

Frontend Development

The UX. Something new, something different. Something that people will find wonderful. It simple, elegant, and lightweight. All. In. One. People have said you can't do both.. well.. apparently that isn't true. New technologies allow for the innovation in the frontend, allowing for better interaction without our computers.

UX Restart

With the revolutionary change in UX design, we can bring forth the age of a better desktop. One that supports applications from all platforms. A change which allows for ignorance to each operating system being different. A change which allows for 99% of games to run well; with more games being ported every single day. The day for the gaming desktop is here too...

Communication

Here is where many other projects have faultered. Not having an open line of communication. I have chose not to hide my name on this project for that very reason. If you find my social media and message me, i will respond (within 24 hours if possible). The best way for me to give the community what they want is by asking them. I don't have the mind of millions and refuse to think I do.

Community Wallpapers

I don't mind having community interaction. That is what it is for! So.. for those which are artistically inclined, we will have polls each month for the top two wallpapers. Each release will include the top two wallpapers from each poll. This makes the process easier on me, and allows for each of you to recognition, as this is your project too! The Sparchunu community owns these wallpapers.
